Welcome

Hello and welcome to my website! I'm a german Game Designer born in '98 who has been working on game projects since 2011 and started extensively studying the subject in 2015. This site lists both my large and independent projects, as well as some of my more recent smaller projects.

Projects

System Sync (2018)
Roles: Artist, Programmer, Level Designer
Team: 3 people
System Sync is a 2D Co-op Puzzle Platformer in which players have to roll, jump and lift each other in order to overcome magnetism-based puzzles. The game features four levels and was developed over the cours of five months.
A Christmas Thing (2017)
Roles: Artist, Programmer, Level Designer
Team: 3 people
A Christmas Thing is a 3D Platformer conceptualised as a christmas present for the YouTube celebrity "raocow". The player takes control of Demo, a cycloptic girl, on her quest to find Super Leeks. The game spans two stages and a boss fight. It serves as a tech demo for a future Unity Engine framework and was developed over the course of three months.
Jungle Clicker (2017)
Roles: Artist, Programmer
Team: 4 people
Jungle Clicker is a clicker game in which the player purchases plants and animals to create an ecosystem on a tree in the rainforest. Plants and animals have unique interactions with each other which stay true to reality, teaching the player about how plants and animals live alongside each other. It was developed over the course of five months.
H2Ω (2017)
Roles: Artist, Programmer, Level Designer
Team: 5 people
H2Ω is a 2D Arcade Game about a security robot who finds his lab flooded and all other robots out of control. Now his goal is to defend himself against his former companions and escape the lab before the water catches up to him. It was developed over the course of five months.
